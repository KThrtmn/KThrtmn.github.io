Personas

persona 1

Kelly is a student and does not have a lot of free time. However, she likes to know what the weather is so she can plan for the day ahead of time. Kelly uses this website to get quick information about the weather.

persona 2

Ken likes to adventure and can be spontaneous. He needs a weather website he can trust to give him information to make informed decisions. He uses this website to get quick information to know if he can go ahead with his plans.

Scenarios

  1. What is the weather tomorow? What should I wear?
  2. Will the weather inhibit my adventures?
  3. Should I stay home or take shelter?
  4. Is school canceled or delayed?
  5. When will the sun rise and set?
